Our Story

We believe construction deserves better tools.

Not enterprise software forced into the field. Not another app that adds work instead of removing it. Something built from the ground up for the way construction actually works.

The origin

KeenSite started with a simple question: why is construction reporting still manual?

The idea came from firsthand experience by CEO Colton Breitkreuz, who previously worked in line painting, pothole repair, and snow removal. On every job, crews were required to fill out detailed reports: when they arrived, when they left, who was on site, how much material was used, and what work was completed.

Most of this was recorded hours later, from memory. This led to wasted time, missing data, billing delays, and constant back-and-forth between the field and the office.

Our mission

KeenSite was built to close that gap.

By combining GPS and software, KeenSite automatically captures job activity as it happens and turns it into clean, verified records. Giving contractors real-time visibility while removing hours of administrative work each week.

The result: fewer errors, faster billing, and a system that finally matches how construction really works.
