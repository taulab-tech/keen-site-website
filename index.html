How it works

Four inputs. One verified record.

Schedule

Who should be where, and when. Planned jobs, assigned crews, expected equipment.

Timecard

What your crew said happened. Hours logged, tasks completed, equipment used.

GPS

Where they actually were. Arrival, departure, and time on site verified by hardware.

Engine Hours

What actually ran. Equipment start and stop times captured automatically.

Verified Record

KeenSite cross-references all four automatically. Everything that lines up gets verified. Everything that doesn’t gets flagged.
