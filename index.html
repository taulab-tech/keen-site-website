How It Works

Four steps. Fully automatic.

Schedule

Crews and equipment are assigned to jobs. The system knows what's planned and where everyone should be.

Field

GPS and engine hour data automatically capture who arrived, when they arrived, what equipment was used, and how long they worked.

Report

Verified GPS and engine data is used to cross-check manually entered timecards, auto-filling key fields and flagging discrepancies.

Invoice

Accurate, verified records flow directly into billing. No disputes, no delays, no reconstructing from memory.

Asset Tracking

Keep track of ALL your equipment.

KeenSite uses a dual-device tracking system designed specifically for construction environments.

GPS Trackers

Vehicles & Large Equipment
  • Installed on trucks, trailers, and heavy machinery
  • Provides real-time GPS tracking
  • Acts as a Bluetooth hub on site
  • Captures movement, usage, and jobsite presence

Bluetooth Beacons

Small Assets & Tools
  • Compact beacons attached to buckets, light posts, trailers, plates, generators, and tools
  • Detected automatically by nearby GPS trackers
  • Tracks asset movement without cellular data
  • Eliminates lost equipment, theft, and jobsite searching

Together, these devices create a complete picture of everything moving across your job sites — from trucks to the smallest tools.
