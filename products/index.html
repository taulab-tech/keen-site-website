Hardware and software built to work together.

KeenSite combines field-ready tracking devices with powerful software so everything that happens on site is automatically captured, verified, and ready to bill.

Hardware

Three devices. Complete site coverage.

Every piece of KeenSite hardware is purpose-built for construction — rugged, low-maintenance, and designed to capture the data that matters without adding work for your crew.

GPS Tracker

Battery

No wiring. No installation crew. Just place it near the engine and Battery gets to work — tracking GPS location, detecting engine hours through vibration, and acting as a Bluetooth hub for nearby tags. Built for equipment that moves between sites and doesn't have a convenient power source.

Deploy on: excavators, skid steers, paint machines, rental equipment

GPS Engine Hours Bluetooth Hub

GPS Tracker

Wired

Hardwired directly to the vehicle's power supply for always-on reliability. Wired captures precise GPS location, engine hours, and kilometres travelled — giving you a complete picture of every truck and machine from ignition to shutdown. No batteries to replace, no gaps in coverage.

Deploy on: trucks, excavators, skid steers

GPS Engine Hours Mileage Bluetooth Hub

Bluetooth Beacon

Tag

Compact and passive — Tag attaches to any asset and broadcasts its presence to nearby GPS trackers automatically. No cellular data, no manual check-ins. When a truck pulls onto site, it picks up every Tag in range and logs what's there. Know exactly where your equipment is without anyone having to look for it.

Deploy on: skid steer attachments, trailers, light towers, generators
